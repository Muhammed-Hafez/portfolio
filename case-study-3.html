Overview

This project streamlined doctor appointment booking, allowing patients to find doctors by location, reviews, and specialty while organizing medical records. It also helped doctors replace manual tracking with a structured dashboard.

Designed the patient landing page and dashboard for seamless doctor browsing, appointment booking, and record management.

Collaborated with another designer on the doctor's dashboard and landing page to ensure a cohesive system.
