Overview

The IsekaiCode landing page redesign improved usability and gave it a modern look by enhancing typography, colors, navigation, and user experience.

Stakeholder feedback and competitor analysis were used to guide decisions in place of direct user research.

The final result was a clearer, more user-friendly page that increased engagement and led to a partnership with another organization.
